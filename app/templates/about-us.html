Our Work

PDOCHEALTH

The pandemic has caused so many inconveniences. One of them is reaching out to a doctor when required. Many hospitals have closed down OPDs and a lot of people are now in need. In this situation pdochealth is a platform that allows people to consult a doctor over video call in your smart devices in just a few clicks. You can receive the doctors’ prescription and diagnosis summary live during the call, take a fully qualified copy of the prescription so that you can conveniently purchase the medicines. Upload old medical records, add more files during the live call if asked for. You can also find diagnostic tests and a lot more services are on their way.

P-SHA

P-SHA (Palm Sized Health Assistant) is here to ease management of lifestyle diseases like Blood Pressure, Diabetes, Apnea etc. Using Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics for reporting thereby reducing the effort in diagnosis and emergency detection. This ground breaking research is on its way to be patented.

Aisana

Imagine AI teaching you Yoga. With the ease of finding a trainer in your free time and also with the accuracy of AI. AI-sana is an upcoming platform by EDOC where you can perform yoga while being guided by an AI in real time. Set your goals and you can perform yoga anytime any day with just a camera enabled device.

Research and Development

Our dedicated R & D team continuously conducts research to build state of the art medical devices using advanced technologies using IOT and Artificial Intelligence. EDOC Technologies

Centralization using software can greatly reduce manual and tedious efforts in the healthcare system.
